# PRODUCT.md — Eli's Hot Sand Surf School

## Register
brand

## Product purpose
A one-page marketing site that turns Tamarindo visitors into booked surf lessons.
The page IS the product: a visitor's gut impression ("this looks warm, real, and
fun, I trust this guy") is the conversion. Primary action is a WhatsApp message;
secondary is an email inquiry form.

## Users
- Tourists in Guanacaste, Costa Rica (mostly English-speaking, some Spanish),
  many first-time surfers, families, couples, solo travelers.
- Browsing on phones, often on patchy connections, often at golden hour deciding
  "what should we do tomorrow."

## Brand voice
Three words: **sun-warmed, easygoing, hand-on-the-shoulder reassuring.**
Talks like a local who genuinely wants you to have a great morning, not a
salesperson. Pura vida, never corporate. Family-friendly and safety-first without
being stiff. Bilingual EN/ES, equal footing.

## Anti-references
- Generic SaaS / startup landing pages (cards-in-a-row, hero-metric template).
- Cold "adventure-tour-operator" templates with stock photos and blue gradients.
- Over-polished luxury-resort coldness. This is warm, sandy, personal.
- Editorial-magazine affectation (serif-italic + ruled columns) — wrong register
  for a beach surf school.

## Strategic principles
- WhatsApp is the conversion. It is always one tap away.
- Real photography carries the page. The assets (golden-hour Tamarindo, Eli
  surfing, real group lessons) are the hero, not decoration.
- Trust signals matter: Google 5.0 rating, real reviews, "most people stand up
  on day one," all equipment included.
- Fast and mobile-first. No build step, hosts on Cloudflare Pages.
