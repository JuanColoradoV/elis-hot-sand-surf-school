How it works

From the sand to your first wave.

  1. 01

    Reach out

    Message us on WhatsApp or grab a time on the calendar. Tell us your level, ages, and dates.

  2. 02

    Meet on the beach

    We bring the soft-top boards, rashguards, and all the gear. Quick warm-up and safety chat on the sand.

  3. 03

    Ride your first wave

    Eli reads every set and guides you into the water. Most first-timers are up and smiling on day one.

Meet your instructor

Hi, I'm Eli.

I've spent my life reading the waves of Playa Grande and Playa Tamarindo, and there's nothing I love more than seeing someone ride their very first wave. My lessons are patient, safety-first, and a whole lot of fun. Most people are standing up by the end of their first session.

If you can stand on the sand, I'll get you standing on a wave.
  • Local knowledge of the best beginner waves & tides
  • Safety-first, family-friendly coaching
  • All equipment provided, just bring sunscreen
  • Lessons in English & Spanish
